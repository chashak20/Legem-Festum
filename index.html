About

Legem Festum is the annual flagship law fest celebrating excellence, innovation, and the evolving spirit of justice. It brings together aspiring legal minds on a dynamic platform featuring academic competition such as legal essay/drafting, debates, legal quizzes, cultural events,sports and client counseling, fostering advocacy, critical thinking, and leadership.
