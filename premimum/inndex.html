About the Fest

The Grand Celebration

Legem Festum is the most prestigious annual law fest, bringing together legal minds, aspiring advocates, and creative souls from across the nation for three exhilarating days of competition, culture, and camaraderie.

From intense legal debates and moot courts to electrifying cultural performances and sporting showdowns, this fest is where intellect meets energy, and tradition meets innovation.
