About The Fest

Legem Festum is a grand celebration of law, justice, and intellectual pursuit — a three-day national level law fest that brings together the brightest legal minds from across the country. From riveting moot courts to electrifying cultural performances, every moment is crafted to inspire.

Join us for an unforgettable confluence of legal excellence, cultural vibrancy, and competitive spirit. Whether you're a budding lawyer, a seasoned debater, or simply someone who revels in the power of words and justice — this fest is your stage.
